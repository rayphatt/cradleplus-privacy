Privacy Policy for Cradle+

Last updated: April 28, 2025

Introduction

Cradle+ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • User ID (for account management)
  • Subscription status and purchase history

Usage Information

  • Product scan history
  • Search queries
  • App interaction data
  • Device information

Camera Usage

  • We use the camera solely for scanning product barcodes
  • We do not store or transmit any images
  • Camera access is required for the core functionality of the app

How We Use Your Information

  1. To provide and maintain our service
  2. To manage your account and subscription
  3. To improve our app and user experience
  4. To communicate with you about your account or subscription
  5. To comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Firebase
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties

Subscription Information

  • Subscription payments are processed through the App Store
  • We store your subscription status to provide access to premium features
  • You can manage your subscription through your App Store account

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access your personal information
  2. Correct inaccurate information
  3. Request deletion of your account
  4. Opt-out of marketing communications
  5. Export your data

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
